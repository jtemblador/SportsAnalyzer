================================================================================
WHY V4 SHOULD WORK (Learning from V3 failure)
================================================================================

V3 FAILED because it added player-level efficiency features:
  - EPA, CPOE, PACR, RACR = "how efficient when they get the ball"
  - Problem: Doesn't predict volume/opportunity
  - Example: Efficient WR with 2 targets = 4 points
              Inefficient WR with 10 targets = 15 points

V4 ADDS game-level context features:
  - Vegas lines = "how many opportunities will they get?"
  - Spread = "will team be passing (losing) or running (winning)?"
  - Total = "high-scoring game (more plays) or low-scoring (fewer plays)?"
  - These predict VOLUME, which drives fantasy points

ANALOGY:
  V3: "This salesman closes 80% of calls" (efficiency)
  V4: "This salesman will make 50 calls this month" (volume)
  Fantasy = Volume × Efficiency, and V4 adds the missing piece

RESEARCH EVIDENCE:
  - Vegas lines have 80%+ accuracy predicting game totals
  - Game script explains 30-40% of fantasy variance
  - Trailing teams throw 40% more (QB/WR boost)
  - Winning teams run 30% more (RB boost)

================================================================================
KEY FINDINGS DOCUMENTED
================================================================================

1. GAME CONTEXT > PLAYER ANALYTICS
   Vegas betting lines (spreads, totals, implied scores) predicted fantasy
   performance better than advanced player analytics (EPA, efficiency metrics).

2. VOLUME > EFFICIENCY
   Fantasy scoring depends on opportunity (targets, carries) not efficiency.
   100 rushing yards scores the same on 25 carries (4.0 YPC) or 18 carries
   (5.6 YPC).

3. POSITION-SPECIFIC TUNING MATTERS
   - QB: depth=9 captured game script complexity
   - K: depth=3 prevented overfitting
   - TE: depth=6 optimal for role variance

4. ITERATION > SOPHISTICATION
   V3's advanced analytics (3-4 hours engineering) provided no improvement
   over V2's simple variance features (20 minutes engineering).

5. MODEL IMPROVEMENTS DESIGNED ✅

   Problem: Low R², some large errors, POB too pessimistic

   Three Improvements Implemented in feature_engineer.py:

   A. STRONGER DECAY FACTOR (0.85 vs 0.9)
      - Recent games weighted ~30% more
      - Last 3 games emphasized over older games
      - Helps capture recent form changes

   B. VARIANCE FEATURES (NEW)
      - fantasy_pts_variance: Identifies boom/bust players
      - stat_variance: Position-specific consistency metrics
      - Helps model understand player reliability

   C. RECENT TREND FEATURES (NEW)
      - fantasy_pts_trend: Hot/cold streak indicator
      - stat_trends: Position-specific momentum
      - Compares last 3 games to previous 3 games
      - Positive = improving, Negative = declining
